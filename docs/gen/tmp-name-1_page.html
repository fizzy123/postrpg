The general idea is that no one expects to be inflitrated by literal clowns. The circus is also an ideal way to set up alibi. If the circus is in one city, but an incident happens across the country, there's a perception that therre's no way the circus could be involved. The use timers, magic, remote control, and psychology to make sure they'll never be suspected. They're often working with a goal of setting up something new, or executing something remotely. Unless they let you know what they're doing, you're fairly unlikely to find out who they really are.
